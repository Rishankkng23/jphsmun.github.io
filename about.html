Madhu Mam's words

MUNning has become extremely popular among students across the globe. It is truly inspiring to see young students discuss such grave issues with so much confidence. A Model UN conference not only hones a student’s talent in public speaking, but it also edifies the skills of compromise, diplomacy, and negotiations. It helps students to develop a healthy interest in world politics and it develops the leaders of tomorrow. As part of its endeavor to provide a holistic approach to education, high school and college students engage in intellectual deliberation, as an exercise to bemore productive and develop strong friendships. Indeed, a Model UN conference enables each student to harness their analytical and problem-solving skills. The United Nations is needed more than ever at this time of multiple crises, increased sectarian violence and the wanton destruction of cultural heritage sites. It is time to recall Gandhiji’s call for peace and reconciliation: - An eye for an eye will make the whole world blind. The conference relays the theme 'Aurea Mediocritas,' which means the Golden Mean or the Golden Middle Way. It represents the desirable middle between two extremes. It symbolizes the importance of negotiations and finding a middle ground as a Golden solution to issues. We believe that the UN is all about finding this balance, the golden mean, and unilateralism is doomed to fail in our highly connected global village. Which is why ‘ Aurea ​Mediocritas’ remains the theme of JPHS MUN 2022.

I wish that each delegate in this prestigious conference stands by the belief that adversities can be changed into opportunities, peaceful protests can accomplish much more than military aggression, counter the forces of intolerance, advance global citizenship, and forge human solidarity based on Mahatma Gandhi’s philosophy of non-violence. Thank you.
